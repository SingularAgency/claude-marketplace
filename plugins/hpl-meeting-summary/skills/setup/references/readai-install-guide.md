# Read AI MCP — Installation Guide

Read AI provides the meeting data this plugin depends on (summaries, action items, transcripts, topics). Without it, no summaries can be generated.

---

## How to install Read AI in Cowork

**Step 1 — Open plugin/connector settings**
In the Cowork desktop app, click the **Plugins** icon in the left sidebar (or go to Settings → Plugins & Connectors).

**Step 2 — Search for Read AI**
In the connector search bar, type "Read AI". Click the Read AI card when it appears.

**Step 3 — Connect your account**
Click **Connect** and sign in with your Read AI account (the same account your meetings are recorded to). Cowork will request read access to your meetings.

**Step 4 — Verify the connection**
Once connected, come back here and say: _"Set up the meeting summary plugin"_ — the setup wizard will automatically verify the connection is working.

---

## Don't have a Read AI account?

1. Go to [https://read.ai](https://read.ai) and sign up (free plan available)
2. Install the Read AI Chrome extension or calendar integration
3. Enable meeting recording for Google Meet, Zoom, or Microsoft Teams
4. Once your first meeting is recorded, come back and install the connector

---

## Troubleshooting

| Error | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| "Unauthorized" after connecting | Re-authenticate by disconnecting and reconnecting in plugin settings |
| No meetings showing | Make sure your meetings are being recorded and processed by Read AI (can take a few minutes after a call ends) |
| Connection times out | Check your internet connection and try reconnecting |
