# Slack MCP — Installation Guide

Slack is where meeting summaries will be posted. Without this connection, summaries can only be viewed in Cowork — not shared with your team.

---

## How to install Slack in Cowork

**Step 1 — Open plugin/connector settings**
In the Cowork desktop app, click the **Plugins** icon in the left sidebar (or go to Settings → Plugins & Connectors).

**Step 2 — Search for Slack**
In the connector search bar, type "Slack". Click the Slack card when it appears.

**Step 3 — Authorize the workspace**
Click **Connect** and sign in with your Slack account. Authorize Cowork to post messages on your behalf. Make sure you're authorizing the correct Slack workspace (e.g., Singular Agency).

**Step 4 — Verify the connection**
Come back here and say: _"Set up the meeting summary plugin"_ — the setup wizard will verify Slack is reachable and let you pick a default channel.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Error | Fix |
|-------|-----|
| "Not authorized" when posting | Re-authenticate by disconnecting and reconnecting Slack in plugin settings |
| Can't find a channel | Make sure you're a member of the channel you're trying to post to |
| Message not showing in Slack | Check that Cowork has permission to post to that channel type (private channels may require additional approval) |
