# Daily Executive Briefing

Pulls live data from Google Calendar, Gmail, Slack, and Airtable, analyzes it through a senior PM lens, and delivers a prioritized, action-oriented daily briefing — with draft responses included.

## Install

```
/plugin install daily-executive-briefing@singular-agency-marketplace
```

## Requirements

- Google Calendar MCP connector must be enabled in your Cowork session.
- Gmail MCP connector must be enabled in your Cowork session.
- Slack MCP connector must be enabled in your Cowork session.
- Airtable MCP connector must be enabled in your Cowork session.

## Skills

| Skill | What it does |
|---|---|
| `daily-executive-briefing` | Aggregates signals from Calendar, Gmail, Slack, and Airtable to generate a structured executive briefing with urgent items, high-value actions, follow-ups, and strategic alerts |

## Example triggers

- "Dame mi briefing de hoy"
- "What's on my plate today?"
- "Give me a daily summary"
- "What should I focus on today?"
- "Prepare my morning briefing and send it to Slack"
