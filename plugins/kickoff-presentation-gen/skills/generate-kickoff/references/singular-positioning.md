# Singular 2026 Positioning

## What We Are

**AI Transformation Partner for SMBs**

## Core Capabilities

- AI transformation strategy
- Co-Pilot™, OpenClaw, Omni Model™
- Claude Orchestrator™
- Agentic AI & automation
- Direction, Taste & Speed
- OKR-driven outcomes & ROI
- Partner → transformational

## Business Model

**Revenue = outcomes × impact**

## Key Differentiators

- **Not a dev shop** — Singular is a transformation partner that delivers measurable business outcomes, not just features
- **OKR-driven delivery** — every engagement is structured around strategic objectives and measurable key results, not hourly billing for tasks
- **AI-first approach** — leverage AI across the entire product lifecycle: strategy, design, development, automation, and operations
- **Direction, Taste & Speed** — the competitive advantages Singular sells are strategic direction, design taste, and execution speed — not developer hours or low cost
- **Outcome-based value** — pricing and engagement models are tied to client outcomes and ROI, not just effort

## Engagement Approach

- Near-shore talent across Latin America, US-based leadership
- Multidisciplinary teams (product owner/digital strategist, designers, developers)
- Bi-weekly agile sprints with transparent project management via Singular Stories
- Product Engine / velocity simulator for timeline and investment modeling
- Slack for day-to-day communication, Google Meet for weekly meetings, Email for invoicing

## How This Affects Kickoff Presentations

The "What we are" slide must reflect the 2026 positioning above — not a generic agency description. Every kickoff should reinforce that Singular is an AI Transformation Partner delivering outcomes and impact, not a traditional development agency.
