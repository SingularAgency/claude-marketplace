Summarize the provided content (selected text, file, or recent code changes) into a clear, structured output.

Follow this format:

## TL;DR
One sentence capturing the single most important point.

## Key Points
- Up to 5 bullet points covering the main ideas or findings
- Each bullet should be self-contained and actionable where relevant

## Details
A short paragraph (2–4 sentences) expanding on anything that needs context.

## Action Items (if applicable)
Any clear next steps or decisions that arise from the content.

Be concise. Avoid unnecessary filler. If the content is code, focus on what it does, any risks or side effects, and what a reviewer should pay attention to.
